Today is the [CSS Naked Day](https://css-naked-day.org/), just like every April 9th. This is the first year I’ve participated after missing it the past two years.

This initiative has been around for 20 years—that’s quite an achievement. I find it interesting because I don’t feel like HTML semantics are in the best shape these days, and an initiative like this helps raise awareness about the issue.

That’s why I’m participating and will continue to do so in the years to come.
